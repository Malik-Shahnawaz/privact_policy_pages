Privacy Policy

Escort Agency Employee Management Applications

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Escort Agency Employee Management applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

Our Applications Include:

  • Escort Agency Admin - Administrative management application
  • Escort Agency Employee - Employee-facing application
  • Escort Agency Manager - Management application

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Information: Name, employee ID, contact details, date of birth
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Employment Information: Job title, department, employment status, work schedule
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, security questions
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for attendance tracking and location-based services
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, performance data

2.2 Sensitive Information

We may collect sensitive information including:

  • Biometric data for authentication purposes
  • Health information related to leave requests
  • Financial information for payroll processing
  • Emergency contact information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Primary Business Functions

  • Employee management and administration
  • Attendance tracking and time management
  • Payroll processing and financial management
  • Leave request processing
  • Performance monitoring and reporting
  • Communication and notifications

3.2 Technical Operations

  • App functionality and feature delivery
  • User authentication and security
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Error tracking and debugging
  • Data backup and recovery

3.3 Legal and Compliance

  • Compliance with employment laws and regulations
  • Legal reporting requirements
  • Audit and investigation purposes
  • Dispute resolution

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Internal Sharing

  • With authorized personnel within your organization
  • With supervisors and managers for legitimate business purposes
  • With HR departments for administrative functions

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Cloud storage and hosting providers
  • Analytics and monitoring services
  • Payment processing services
  • Communication service providers

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To ensure user safety and security
  • In case of business transfers or mergers

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure data storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure backup and recovery procedures

5.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Access limitation and monitoring
  • Regular policy reviews and updates
  • Incident response procedures

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Specifically:

  • Employment Records: Retained for the duration of employment plus 7 years
  • Payroll Information: Retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Attendance Records: Retained for 3 years
  • App Usage Data: Retained for 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Communication Records: Retained for 1 year

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Obtain a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Review the information we have about you

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Update your contact details and preferences
  • Modify your account settings

7.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Object to certain uses of your information

8. Location Services

Our applications may use location services for the following purposes:

  • Attendance Tracking: To verify employee location during check-in/check-out
  • Geofencing: To ensure employees are at designated work locations
  • Emergency Services: To provide location data in case of emergencies
  • Route Optimization: To optimize travel routes for field employees
Note: Location services can be disabled in your device settings, but this may affect certain app functionalities.

9. Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate with third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • Firebase: For analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications
  • Google Services: For maps, authentication, and cloud services
  • Payment Processors: For financial transactions
  • Communication Services: For SMS and email notifications

10. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information.

11. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending notifications through the app
  • Email notifications to registered users
  • In-app notifications and prompts

Your continued use of our applications after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Compliance and Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)
  • Other applicable local and international privacy laws
